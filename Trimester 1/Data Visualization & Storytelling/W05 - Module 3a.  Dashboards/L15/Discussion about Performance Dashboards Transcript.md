Discussion about Performance Dashboards Transcript 
 
Now, we will be discussing performance dashboards. Performance dashboards are a key 
subtype of dashboards that are used by the organisations to execute their strategy, 
implement their strategy and get the desired results. In this process, they would build a 
dashboard that would capture the strategic goals into actionable items and identify reasons 
for any gaps or course corrections in an objective and transparent manner. 
  
The usefulness of performance dashboards is well recognised in the business and industry 
where almost all processes have performance dashboards that are constantly tracked by the 
lowest level of the management to the topmost level depending upon where they sit in the 
management framework and use these cues from the performance dashboards to correct the 
cache. So, what are performance dashboards key attributes? The performance dashboards 
enable strategy execution. A strategy is formulated in the board or the company besides this 
wants to grow by 20% in revenue or it wants to increase its footfall in say in 10 new regions. 
  
The plan is drawn, then it is broken into small elements and responsibilities are assigned to 
different groups in the organisation and the metrics are also decided on how to evaluate the 
performance and see what are the gaps on a real-time basis. These elements are brought 
together in a performance dashboard. First, it enables monitoring of the strategic objectives 
and it also enables a collaboration because in real time when you have a dashboard you can 
make the parties across the table. 
  
For example, there is a supplier group and there is a user group or a product development 
group and a testing group so they can interact with each other using a dashboard in a very 
collaborative fashion with clearly identified metrics without gap for misunderstanding or 
miscommunication. Then it also enables most importantly corrections. The objective is not to 
find fault but to create a pathway to correct so that the overall organisation or particular 
process moves as per the plan. 
  
Then corrections can be enabled looking at the gaps. Finally, it is very objective. Before 
designing the dashboard, the performance dashboard, you would decide on what are the key 
objective metrics that you would be using to decide on the performance. 
  
So the discussion would have an a priori to decide what are the objective metrics and it is very 
transparent therefore because everybody knows these are the metrics on which the 
performance will be measured and if there are gaps what actions have to be taken so that 
overall strategic objective is executed in the given time frame. So with these characteristics, 
performance dashboards are a key managerial tool for people to smoothly execute these 
strategies. Now let us see how a performance dashboard is constituted or what are the key 
elements of a performance dashboard.
