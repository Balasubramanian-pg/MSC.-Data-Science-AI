### **Your Learning Path:  
****From Raw Data To Explainable Insights**

**Welcome!** This roadmap outlines your journey through the world of Data Preprocessing. Each phase builds on the last, helping you move from understanding the basics of data science to preparing clean, reduced, and explainable datasets for real-world applications.  
  

**Phase 1: Laying the Foundations (Week 1–2)**

In this phase, you’ll step into the world of data science and data mining. You’ll discover what datasets are, how attributes work, and why preprocessing is the backbone of successful analytics.

- **Week 1:** Introduction to Data Science and Mining  
    **You’ll learn:** The role of data preprocessing in the larger data science lifecycle, the concept of KDD, and key methods of data mining.
- **Week 2:** Understanding Data  
    **You’ll learn:** To classify data types, understand attributes, and recognise how raw data is represented, measured, and sourced.

**Phase 2: Data Challenges and Cleaning (Week 3–4)**

Here, you’ll identify common data quality issues and learn practical cleaning and integration techniques. This phase makes raw data usable and trustworthy.

- **Week 3:** Data Quality and Issues  
    **You’ll learn:** How to detect poor data quality, address missing/duplicate values, and understand the difference between noise and outliers.
- **Week 4:** Data Cleaning and Integration  
    **You’ll learn:** Techniques for cleaning messy data, handling inconsistencies, and integrating multiple data sources while resolving redundancies.

**Phase 3: Transforming and Reducing Data (Week 5–6)**

After cleaning, you’ll focus on transforming and optimising datasets to improve performance and efficiency.

- **Week 5:** Data Transformation  
    **You’ll learn:** Normalization methods, aggregation strategies, and transformations that prepare data for better analysis.
- **Week 6:** Data Reduction  
    **You’ll learn:** To minimise data complexity while retaining essential patterns using dimensionality reduction, sampling, and feature engineering.

**Phase 4: Analysing Similarity & Ensuring Explainability (Week 7–8)**

In the final phase, you’ll explore how to measure similarities across data and ensure transparency in preprocessing decisions.

- **Week 7:** Proximity Measures  
    **You’ll learn:** To calculate similarity/distance between data points using various measures, which is crucial for clustering and classification.
- **Week 8:** Data Explainability  
    **You’ll learn:** How preprocessing affects data distribution, interpret variance and skewness, handle outliers responsibly, and understand feature importance.

**Your Destination**

By the end of this journey, you will be able to:

- Detect and resolve **data quality issues**.
- Apply **cleaning, integration, transformation, and reduction** techniques.
- Use **proximity measures** to analyse data similarities.
- Ensure **data explainability** for transparent and fair analysis.

You’ll be prepared to transform raw datasets into **high-quality, reliable inputs** for machine learning and decision-making systems.
